"Decreasing number of Drug/Narcotic incidents in San Francisco from 2003-2017"
permalink: /https://miedupont.github.io/Assignment2/webpage
The number of Drug/Narcotic incidents in San Fransico has been decreasing from 2003-2017. This page will analyze and visualize the data of Drug/Narcotic incidents in San Fransico from 2003-2017 and try to find possible reasons for why we see this downward trend in the number of incidents.
